Space Tilt Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Space Tilt ("we", "us", or "our"). We respect the privacy of our users ("user" or "you") and are committed to protecting any information necessary for the functionality of our motion-controlled arcade game. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding device data usage and storage.

Information We Use

Space Tilt is designed with privacy in mind and uses minimal device data:

  • Motion Sensor Data: We access your device's motion sensors solely for gameplay functionality (tilt controls). This data is processed in real-time and is not stored or transmitted.
  • Local Storage: All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases for cosmetic items, the transactions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Internet Connectivity

Space Tilt requires internet connectivity only for:

  • Processing in-app purchases through the App Store
  • Downloading any future game updates

Data Storage

All game data, including:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Settings preferences
  • Purchased cosmetic items
  • Motion calibration settings

is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. All game data is stored locally on the device and no personal information is collected or transmitted.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
