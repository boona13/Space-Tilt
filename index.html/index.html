Space Tilt Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 28, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Space Tilt ("we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and collect only the minimal data required for our motion‑controlled arcade game to function. This policy explains what we use and why.

Information We Use

  • Motion Sensor Data: We access the accelerometer/gyroscope strictly for gameplay (tilt controls). This data is processed in real time and is not stored or transmitted.
  • Leaderboard Username: If you submit a score to the online leaderboard, we store your chosen username solely to display your ranking.
  • Device Identifier (UUID): We generate a device‑only identifier (stored in the iOS Keychain) to enable offline‑first sync and prevent duplicate submissions. It is used only for app functionality and is not used for advertising or tracking across apps.
  • Local Game Data: Your high scores, coin count, settings, and purchased cosmetics are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchases: In‑app purchases are processed by Apple’s App Store (StoreKit). We never see or store payment information.

Internet Connectivity

Space Tilt connects to the internet only to:

  • Process in‑app purchases via Apple’s StoreKit
  • Submit and fetch leaderboard scores and usernames
  • Download app updates

Advertising & Tracking

We do not use the iOS Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and do not track users across apps or websites. If ads are shown, they are configured as non‑personalized and limited to general‑audience content.

Third‑Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit: Handles in‑app purchases securely on your device.
  • Supabase: Hosts the online leaderboard (stores submitted username and score only).

We do not use third‑party analytics or data brokers.

Data We Don’t Collect

  • Personal contact information
  • Precise location data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Cross‑app tracking or profiling data

Children’s Privacy

Space Tilt is family‑friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game stores data locally and uses minimal online services as described above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the date above and publish the revised policy here.
